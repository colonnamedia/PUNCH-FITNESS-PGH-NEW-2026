Good to Know

Common Questions.

Do I need to be in shape to join?
Not at all. Senior Sluggers is built for all levels — even complete beginners.
Does boxing help with Parkinson's disease?
Boxing has been shown to help many people with Parkinson's improve their balance, hand-eye coordination, mental focus, muscle strength, and body rhythm.
What should I bring?
Comfortable workout clothes, water, and a great attitude. We provide gloves and wraps for your first class.
Is it safe for people with arthritis or joint issues?
Yes. Our low-impact format and expert coaching help you move safely while improving strength and flexibility.
Are you a Rock Steady Boxing affiliate?
We are not a Rock Steady Boxing affiliate. However, we offer a low-impact boxing fitness program that can support many of the same physical goals often recommended for people living with Parkinson's — controlled movement, rhythm, coordination, posture, and balance. Always consult your physician or care provider before starting any exercise program.
