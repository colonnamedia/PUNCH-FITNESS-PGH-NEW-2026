Parent Questions

What Parents Ask Us.

What ages do you work with?
Youth boxing is for ages 7–17, grouped so kids train with a similar age and skill level.
Is it safe? Do they spar?
Our youth program is non-contact fitness and technique. Kids learn footwork, combinations, and defense on bags and pads.
Does my child need experience?
No. Most kids start with none. Coaches teach fundamentals from day one.
What should they bring?
Athletic clothes, sneakers, and a water bottle. We provide gloves for the first class.
