Do I need my own gloves to start?
No. Gloves are provided for your first class. Most members get their own once they know they are sticking with it.
What size gloves should I get?
For fitness boxing, 12oz–16oz suits most adults. Ask a coach and we will size you in person.
Do I need hand wraps too?
Yes — wraps protect your hands and wrists inside the glove. The 7-Day Starter Pack includes both gloves and wraps to keep.
Can I buy gear at the gym?
Yes. Ask any coach at the front desk for current gear and pricing.
